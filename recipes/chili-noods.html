Chili Oil Noodles

This looks pretty good. Lets get down to it, what would we need and how do we do it?

Ingredients

  • Thick wheat noodles
  • Garlic
  • Chili flakes
  • Light & dark soy sauce, salt, and sugar
  • Neutral Oil
  • Cilantro and green onions
  • Chinese black vinegar and sesame oil
    •

    Method

    1. Cook noodles and place in a bowl when done.
    2. Add garlic, chili flakes, white parts of green onion, light & dark soy sauce, chinese black vinegar, salt, and sugar over the noodles.
    3. Heat oil in a sauce pan until sizzling hot. Pour hot oil over noodles with chili flakes and garlic. Mix noodles, enjoy!
