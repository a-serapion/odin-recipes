Vietnamese Iced Coffee

This looks pretty good. Lets get down to it, what would we need and how do we do it?

Ingredients

  • Ice (If you want it cold)
  • Vietnamese coffee grounds (I use the Nguyen Coffee Brand)
  • Condensed milk
    •

    Method

    1. Boil water. While boiling, put 3 table spoons of your coffee grounds into the dripper.
    2. Fill dripper with boiling water and let sit for 15-20 minutes.
    3. Walk dog if you have one.
    4. Add 2 tablespoons of condensed milk to cup and pour the steeped coffee into cup.
    5. Mix and enjoy! :)
