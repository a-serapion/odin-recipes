Carbonara

This looks pretty good. Lets get down to it, what would we need and how do we do it?

Ingredients

  • 3 to 4 Eggs
  • 1/2 pound of Pancetta
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan
  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil
  • Kosher Salt
    •

    Method

    1. Heat pasta water with large pot and a pinch of salt
    2. Sautee the Pancetta in a pan over medium heat until crispy. You could add garlic if you want! You want to make sure as much of the fat turned oil stays in the pan.
    3. Beat the eggs and half of the cheese
    4. Cook the pasta
    5. Toss the pasta with the pancetta in the pan. You want to ensure that the pasta is al dente here.
    6. With the residual heat, add the beatn egg mixture.
    7. Serve after rest with more parmesan and ground pepper.
